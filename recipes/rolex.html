Ugandan Rolex

Directions

Ingredients

  • 2 East African Chapatis
  • 2 medium or large Eggs
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1/2 green paper
  • Salt - to your liking

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a cup or bowl
  2. Chop the greenpaper and drop them into the eggs
  3. Put a pinch of salt into the cup
  4. Beat the eggs until the liquid is fairly loose
  5. Fry the egg into an ommelete with medium heat
  6. Place one of your chapitis down and put the omelette on it
  7. Slice your tomato into circular slices and place them on the egg one next to the other
    8. Your can add a source of your choice(mayonaise, ketchup, etc)
  8. Place the other chapati on top of the egg and roll this masterpiece
